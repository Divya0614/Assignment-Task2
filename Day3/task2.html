AI REVOLUTION IN HEALTHCARE

Artificial Intelligence is playing a major role in healthcare today. From faster diagnostics to personalized treatments, AI is helping doctors and patients make better decisions. Hospitals are now using AI systems to detect diseases at an early stage with higher accuracy.

AI is also improving patient care with chatbots, virtual health assistants, and smart monitoring devices. These tools help doctors track patients remotely and give timely advice. However, experts also warn about privacy and data security challenges that must be addressed.

Related Stories

Robotics in surgery

Robots are now assisting doctors in performing surgeries with high precision. These robotic systems reduce human error, allow minimally invasive procedures, and help patients recover faster.

Wearbale Health Devices

Smartwatches and fitness trackers are saving lives by detecting irregular heartbeats, monitoring blood pressure, and even alerting emergency contacts during accidents. The popularity of such devices is rising worldwide

Telemedicine Growth

Virtual consultations are becoming a normal part of healthcare. Patients can now speak with doctors from the comfort of their homes, making healthcare more accessible, especially in rural areas.
